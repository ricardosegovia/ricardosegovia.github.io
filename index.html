My research has aimed to identify structural features of biodiversity to understand evolutionary and biogeographic processes. I'm currently an associate researcher in the Institute of Ecology and Biodiversity (Chile).

In addition, I'm interested in the scientific study of the relationship between societies and environments in global and local scales. As a member of the Chilean community of scientists, I'm also interested in the modernization of the science policy of my country.

Ongoing Projects.

  • Chilean herbaria digitization
    In collaboration with BIODATA team, we are addresing the digitation of the Chilean herbaria.
  • Machine Learning to state the global structure of Biodiversity
    In collaboration with Diego Arenas from DFKI, The German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence, we are working in this project funded by AI for Earth, Microsoft (2019-2021).
  • Southern South American forest plots
    I am building a network of forest plots in Chile that will be integrated with collaborators in the Americas to study variation in species richness at broad scales.
